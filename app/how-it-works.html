The Block loop

Each step exists to reduce planning overhead and increase quality touches in the field.

1) Import / organize leads

Bring leads into Block and normalize the basics: address, status, and who owns it.

2) Build territories

Create territories you can understand at a glance—balanced and practical for a rep's day.

3) Generate daily routes

Turn territories into daily plans that minimize dead travel and keep reps in a rhythm.

4) Assign & execute

Assignments are clear. Reps update status in the flow of work—fast on mobile.

5) Review & improve

Owners review outcomes quickly: what worked, what didn't, and what to adjust next week.

Design principles

Block is intentionally simple. Field teams win when the system is obvious.

Vertical-first, one idea per screen

Reps and owners get clarity fast—without wide dashboards that hide what matters.

Fewer steps, fewer handoffs

The workflow reduces “office ↔ rep” coordination loops that eat your day.

Reality-based accountability

Progress is visible because it's part of doing the work, not a separate reporting task.

Built for the field

Quick actions, clear states, and short inputs—optimized for phones.
