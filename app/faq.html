Product

What is Block?

Block is a sales operations platform for door-to-door, field-sales, and service businesses. It replaces spreadsheets with a single workflow for routes, lead status, assignments, and owner visibility.

What does “territory” mean in Block?

Territory = the radius / coverage area around a rep manager (not just a polygon on a map). It defines where a rep should work so routes stay tight and repeatable.

Is Block just a route optimizer?

No. Route suggestions are a major differentiator, but Block is about the full workflow: assignment, lead status, follow-ups, and accountability.

Do I need a call to try Block?

No. The demo is available first with a dummy account and dummy territory data. Try it before any call or signup.

Results

How does Block improve close rate?

Better routes reduce dead travel time and keep reps focused in tighter neighborhoods, increasing quality conversations per day. Block also strengthens follow-up discipline so leads don't get lost.

How does Block reduce overhead?

By removing manual handoffs between office and reps. Status, ownership, and next steps live in one system so coordination doesn't become a second job.

What makes spreadsheets fail at scale?

They require manual updates, manual routing, and manual accountability. That creates gaps: low-quality routes, lost follow-ups, and slacking that stays hidden.

Trial

What happens after the demo?

If Block fits, you book a call for onboarding. After onboarding, you get a 1-month free trial to run Block in real routes with your team.

How long does onboarding take?

Most teams can start running initial territories quickly, then refine in the first week. The goal is fast launch, then optimization.

How does pricing work?

Pricing depends on team size and usage. We quote during the call so it matches your reality and avoids surprise add-ons.
