Product & fit

Is Block a route optimization tool or a CRM?

Block is a sales operations platform. Route optimization is a major differentiator, but Block also covers territories, assignments, lead status, follow-ups, and owner-grade accountability.

Who is Block best for?

Business owners running door-to-door, field-sales, or service businesses where territories and daily routes matter. It's designed for teams that don't sit at desks.

Do I need to stop using spreadsheets on day one?

No. Most teams transition in phases. We start by running routes and assignments in Block, then move the remaining tracking out of spreadsheets once the team is comfortable.

Do you support power washing?

Yes. Power washing is a strong fit because neighborhood density and repeat exposure matter. Block keeps territories tight, follow-ups clean, and activity visible.

Results

How does Block improve close rate?

Better routes reduce dead time and keep reps focused in tighter neighborhoods, increasing quality conversations per day. Block also improves follow-up discipline and visibility so deals don't get lost.

What does “nothing falls through cracks” mean in practice?

Every lead has a clear status, owner, and next step. Notes and follow-ups live with the lead, so you don't rely on memory or a separate personal list.

Will my team actually use it?

Block is built for phone-first use: short inputs, clear states, and fast updates. Adoption improves when reps don't feel like they're doing extra reporting.

Trial, onboarding, and pricing

What happens after I book a call?

We confirm fit, map Block to your current workflow, then help onboard your team. After onboarding, you get a 1‑month free trial to run Block in real routes.

How long does onboarding take?

Most teams can start running initial territories quickly, then refine over the first week. The goal is fast launch, then optimization—without disruption.

How does pricing work?

Pricing depends on team size and usage. We quote during the call so it matches your reality and avoids surprise add-ons.
