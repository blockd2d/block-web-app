Key concepts (quick glossary)

Sales operations

The systems and processes that make reps productive: routes, assignments, follow-ups, and reporting.

Territories

Defined areas that reduce travel and improve focus. Good territories increase touches per day.

Routes

The ordered plan a rep follows on a day. Better routes = less drive time and more conversations.

Close rate

The percentage of conversations that turn into customers. Route quality and follow-up discipline affect it.
